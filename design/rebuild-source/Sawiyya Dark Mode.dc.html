👤حسابي
Rules for the dark pass: never pure black — the base is a deep desaturated teal so shadows still read. The primary teal and success green each brighten one step for contrast on dark; gold and coral stay, since they already pass. Elevation is shown with lighter surfaces, not heavier shadows. The camera view is the one place light and dark barely differ — it was always dark.
