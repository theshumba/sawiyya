How Sawiyya feels & sounds

A small, warm sound vocabulary — never a game-show buzzer. Every cue pairs an audio shape with a haptic pattern so the feedback lands even in silence. Tap any card to play it (and feel it, on a phone that supports vibration).

Respects the system, always
Silent-mode mutes all audio but haptics stay on — feedback never disappears. Reduce Motion also softens sound to a single quiet tick, and a dedicated Sound & Haptics toggle in Settings lets learners turn either off independently.
Built for a Deaf-first audience
Because many users won't hear the cues, haptics and on-screen motion carry the full message on their own — sound is an enhancement, never the only signal. Every audio event has a matching vibration and a visible animation.
