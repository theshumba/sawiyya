No video ever leaves your device

Our AI camera analysis happens entirely on your processor. We never stream, record, or upload your live camera feed to the cloud. Your home stays private.

Sawiyya is built on a "Local First" philosophy. We don't ask for your email, your name, or your location. Since we don't store your data, it can't be stolen.

To help you grow, we save your lesson progress, earned XP, and small localized samples of successful signs to refine the AI's understanding of your unique hand movements. This stays encrypted in your app storage.
