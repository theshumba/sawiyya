Privacy, Kindness, and Togetherness

Designed to be safe, supportive, and focused on your family connection. مصمم ليكون آمنًا وداعمًا ومركزًا على ترابط عائلتكم.

Everything runs on your device

كل شي يتم على جهازك

Your video never leaves your phone. Sawiyya processes your signs locally, ensuring your family's privacy is always the top priority. No cloud, no recordings, just learning.

You teach it YOUR hands

أنت اللي تعلّم الكاميرا يدك

The AI adapts to you. By "dropping" your unique signs into our digital Majlis bowl, the system learns how you specifically move, making it easier for every family member to participate.

It grades with kindness

يقيّمك بكل لطافة

We don't use red "X" marks or negative buzzers. Sawiyya celebrates every attempt with gold glows and warm feedback, because learning together should always feel joyful.

It knows its limits

يعرف حدوده

The AI is great at spotting letters and single words. It won't try to guess complex poetry or fast sentences yet—we believe in being honest about what the tech can do today.

You're always right about your hands

أنت أبخص بيدك

Technology isn't perfect, but you are the expert of your own home. If the AI misses a sign you know you did correctly, just tap the "I signed it right" button. We trust you over the computer.

Building Bridges with Dignity

Sawiyya is a bridge between the hearing and the Deaf world, built with Gulf values of respect and family warmth.
