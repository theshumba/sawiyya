Privacy is our foundation as a Mada Innovation Award winner. We built Sawiyya to teach, not to track.

videocam_off

No video ever leaves your device

Sawiyya uses advanced local machine learning to recognize your signs. Your camera feed is processed in real-time in temporary memory and is never uploaded, stored, or shared with any server.

shield_with_heart

No account, no server — nothing to leak

We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number. Your progress is tied to your physical device. If we don't have your data, it can't be stolen. This is our "Privacy by Design" promise.

What we keep on your device

To provide the best learning experience, we store a tiny amount of data strictly on your phone:

  • 1 Progress data: Unlocked signs, XP, and your daily streak.
  • 2 Local Cache: Temporary files to make the app run faster.

Erase everything

You are in total control. One tap clears all your progress, XP, and settings from this device. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
